privacy policy - smooth solitaire

updated: 2025-12-25

overview

smooth solitaire is a single-player card game that respects your privacy. we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

data collection

we do not collect any data. the app:

  • does not require an account or login
  • does not collect personal information
  • does not use analytics or tracking
  • does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • does not connect to external servers

local storage

game settings and statistics are stored locally on your device using ios standard storage. this data:

  • never leaves your device
  • is not accessible to us or any third party
  • can be deleted by uninstalling the app

third-party services

smooth solitaire does not integrate with any third-party services, sdks, or advertising networks.

children's privacy

this app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

changes

if we update this policy, changes will be posted here with an updated date.

contact

questions? privacypolicy@cjwinslow.com
