Privacy Policy

Effective: December 2024

Overview

Stonks Rookie ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Game Progress: Portfolio state, completed scenarios, and achievements (stored locally on your device)
  • Preferences: Settings like sound/haptic preferences (stored locally)

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Third-party advertising data

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs via your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Period.

Children's Privacy

Stonks Rookie is designed for users 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Removing app data from Settings > General > iPhone Storage

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
